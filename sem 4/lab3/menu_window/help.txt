================= Game Guide =================

The game "Checkers" is a classic board game.

Game Rules:

Two players compete on a 8x8 board.
The objective is to capture all opponent's pieces or block them from making a move.
Pieces move diagonally to empty squares.
You can capture an opponent's piece by jumping over it.
When a piece reaches the opposite end of the board, it becomes a king and can move in any direction.

Controls:
Use the mouse to select and move pieces.
Click on your piece and then on the square where you want to move.
Press ESCAPE to return to the main menu.
